Information

Welcome to LinkAge+. This app is designed to make your life easier, whether you're an old person in need, or someone who cares for one. Simply put, you can send us your details at any time in case some sort of emergency or unfortunate situation occurs.

By asking you some of your details, you can then speedily and easily submit that data and the appopriate body/individual will come in contact with you as soon as possible. The incidents you with to report can be classified as emergency or non-emergency.

There can be urgent calls and non-urgent calls. An urgent call will be fulfilled within 24h time.
